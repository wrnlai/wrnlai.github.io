PredictEV

Date

August '18 - January '19

Position

UI/UX Designer

Background

PredictEV is a blockchain-powered social network focused on sports predictions and related predictive content. PredictEV upends the traditional social network paradigm by allowing content producers (and consumers!) to directly benefit from popular and accurate content on the network.

Challenge

The company envisioned a mobile experience where users could cast sports bets through a live stream while providing commentary, similar to how a regular sportscaster would. As the sole designer, I was tasked with designing this mobile experience.

Skills

  • User Experience Design

  • Prototyping

  • Mobile Interface Design

1. Research

Secondary Research

To get a better idea of the current live-streaming platforms, I studied popular livestream video apps like TikTok, Instagram Live, and Periscope. In particular, I observed the way in which live-streamers could interact with their viewers.

Insights

  1. All other live-streaming services placed their interface elements to the borders of the pages in order to focus the viewer on the streamer. In some cases, some apps would utilize elaborate drop down and swipe-up interactions as a way to conceal.
  2. The chat functionality was the main communicative tool where live streamers and viewers interacted. Streamers could either respond audibly through the stream or send a message.

2.
Initial Sketches and Concepts

Drawing from my insights from current live streaming apps, I sketched out some initial concepts on how I might incoroporate sports betting into live-streaming.

3.
Interface Design

Onboarding


The onboarding was designed to allow live streamers to search and find the game that they wanted to discuss about. From there, they were given the option to make an initial prediction or skip right to live streaming.

Making a Prediction


Clicking on the Purple PEV button opens up the prediction modal, displaying all of the betting options for that selected game. Live-streamers have an option to make any number of bets.
