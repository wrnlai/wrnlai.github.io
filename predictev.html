PredictEV

Date

August '18 - January '19

Position

UI/UX Designer

Background

PredictEV is a blockchain-powered social network focused on sports predictions and related predictive content. PredictEV upends the traditional social network paradigm by allowing content producers (and consumers!) to directly benefit from popular and accurate content on the network.

Challenge

Design the user interface and experience for both streamers and viewers.

Skills

  • User Experience Design

  • Prototyping

  • Mobile Interface Design

Table of Contents

1 Research 2 Initial Ideas and Concepts 3 High Fidelity Screens 4 Reflections + Lessons Learned

1. Research

Before designing any interfaces, I spent some time conducting secondary research, looking at popular livestream video apps and online betting platforms. In addition, I studied the design language of the current web platform to grab elements and components that I could use to translate over to mobile.

2.
Initial Sketches and Concepts

The company wanted to make their platform more video-centric, to increase community engagement and elicit a sense of excitement for live-stream betting. They envisioned this by having users to broadcast themselves via video while also making in-game predictions during sporting events.

3.
High Fidelity Prototype

Keeping the non-linear search experience in mind, I emphasized an experience that was easily filterable, and forgiving with mistakes.
To communicate these functions in a personable way, I thought of different variations of guiding text to simulate expertise and calm.
