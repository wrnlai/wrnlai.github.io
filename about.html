Projects About In the Lab

I am a fourth-year at the University of California, Berkeley studying data science and design innovation. Unlike many people in my field, I didn’t immediately start studying design in school. Prior to my junior year, I was studying Biology, with the intention of working in healthcare. In addition, I was heavily invested in teaching and research in different communities in the Bay Area. Although I had no experience designing, I was driven to learn as much as possible about human-centered-design through various clubs and electives at Cal.


Fast forward two years and I’ve found a passion for all things related to design. Design lends an opportunity for me to impact positive change to individuals, creatively and visually. My experiences working in healthcare and education allow me to create with empathy, and my background in data science allows me to impact change that is tangible and data-informed.


When I’m not designing, I’m reading about artists and designers ranging from all disciplines or fabricating different items in the Jacobs Makerspace. I love making things with my hands and transforming them.


I’m set to graduate May of 2020 and I am currently seeking full-time roles designing for empowering experiences. Let me know if you’d like to chat!
